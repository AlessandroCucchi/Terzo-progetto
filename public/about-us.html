TONGUE

We turn endless scrolling into moments of meaningful information.

Founders Anaya Deshmukh and Mateo Alon Rivera

Tongue started on Instagram, founded by a journalist and a YouTuber with one goal: to make news fast, clear, and accessible—speaking the language of social media. Today, we’re a growing company with over 50 team members and a community of nearly 3 million followers.

Tongue is a new way to do journalism. Closer. Faster. Human.
